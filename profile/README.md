## How These AI Roleplay Apps Work

Platforms generally leverage powerful language models (custom or API-connected) featuring:

- **Character definitions** — Detailed backstories, traits, and dialogue styles from users or the community
- **Context memory** — Keeps track of history for seamless, continuing stories
- **Multimedia integration** — Generates images or videos to depict scenes and enhance immersion
- **Scenario modes** — Lets you tweak tone, response length, and narrative direction
- **Customization tools** — Modify personas, add specifics, or bring in external models

Available on web and apps, with many providing free access and premium options for quicker replies or advanced features.
